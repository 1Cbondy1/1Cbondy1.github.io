Justo

Project III of my web-development certification at UT was Justo - A fairer freelance app. It required two slightly different user flows, one for employers and one for freelancers. After roles are authenticated at login, only relevant information would be displayed for each role. This wireframe assumes that freelancers would primarily be working at a desktop or laptop, while employers would primarily be using the app on a mobile device.

Grochérie

Project II of my web-development certification at UT was Grochérie - A web application that allows multiple users to view, edit, and complete shopping lists shared across devices. Upon signup and login, the user is presented with a single page consisting of three tabs: Manage, Shop, and Sign out. The Manage tab allows users to perform CRUD actions on lists and items within lists, as well as giving users access to lists. The Shop tab serves as a checklist to be used while shopping.
