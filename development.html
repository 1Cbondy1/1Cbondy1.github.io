Ground

A mobile responsive web application that connects local businesses & restaurants with users looking to recycle excess coffee grounds.

Justo

A React app that acts as the go-between for businesses and freelancers. It's robust tracking systems allow for clear real-time information regarding rates, hours, payment/invoicing, and important dates. - keeping all parties on the same timeline and ensuring that fair work is rewarded with fair pay.

Grochérie

A web application that allows multiple users to view, edit, and complete shopping lists shared across devices. Actions performed are updated in real-time without having to refresh the app. Once lists are created, they can only be accessed by users with whom they have been shared.

Frinder

A full-stack web application that takes in results from a user survey and, using a linear regression algorithm, finds the most compatible friend from arrays of user submitted survey answers.

Handle Burger

Handle Burger allows users to input the names of burgers they'd like to eat. Whenever the user submits a burger's name, it's displayed on the left side of the page. When the button is clicked, the burger will move to the right side of the page.

News Scraper

This app uses Mongoose and Cheerio to scrape news from the NPR website. Users can view and leave comments on the latest news. Each scraped article and associated notes are then saved to the application database.

Texas Trivia

A timed trivia game website. Click the start button to begin. You have one minute to select your answers and complete the quiz. Click the restart button to clear your choices and play again!

Crystals Collector

There are four crystals to choose from. By clicking a crystal, you will be adding a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your score to the randomly generated number. You lose the game if your score goes above that number.

GifTastic

Click a button to add animal gifs from Giphy! Input a new animal into the form to add a new button. Click on an image to animate it and click again for a still image.
