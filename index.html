UX DESIGNER + DEVELOPER

I’ve always had a strong interest in design and technology. These interests are what drew me to LSU's School of Architecture, where I learned the fundamentals of visual and spacial design. It’s also where I picked up my first coding chops through a minor in Digital Media Arts. I took the concepts I learned into practice, as a scenic designer for broadcast television in New York City. After working for a few years, I felt the pull towards a career in an emerging and faster-paced industry.


I moved back to Austin and enrolled in The Coding Bootcamp UT. The content of the course was instantly fascinating to me. The Adobe Creative Suite tools that I've used for years can now be supplemented with JavaScript, React, and other technologies to add functionality to static designs. I hope to use background in design along with the technical knowledge I've gained to jumpstart my career in UX design.
