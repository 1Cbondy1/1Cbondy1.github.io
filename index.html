Developer + Designer

I’ve always had a strong interest in design and technology. These interests are what drew me to LSU's School of Architecture, where I was conditioned to work in an intense, collaborative studio environment. It’s also where I picked up my first coding chops through a minor in digital media arts. I took the concepts I learned into practice, as a scenic designer for broadcast television in New York City. After working for a few years, I felt the pull towards a career in an emerging and faster-paced industry.


I moved back to Austin and enrolled in The Coding Bootcamp UT. The content of the course was instantly fascinating to me. The Adobe Creative Suite tools that I've used for years can now be supplemented with JavaScript, React, and other technologies to add functionality to static designs. I hope to use this technical knowledge along with my background in design to jumpstart my career in UX/UI design and front-end development.
